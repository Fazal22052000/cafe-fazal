Our Story

A Culinary Landmark
in Malad East

Nestled near the Noorani Masjid on Pathanwadi Road, Cafe Fazal has become one of Malad East's most cherished dining destinations. For years we have served the community with rich Mughlai flavours, sizzling tandoori and hearty North Indian cuisine.

With over 500 glowing reviews and a 4.1-star rating, guests return for our consistent quality, generous portions, fresh ingredients — and the warmth that makes every visit feel like home.

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

What are your opening hours?+

We are open 7 days a week from 6:30 AM to 12:30 AM — that is 18 hours every single day, including public holidays and weekends. No day off!

Do you offer home delivery?+

Yes! We deliver via Zomato and Swiggy. You can also call us directly at +91 9920599891 for telephone orders in the local Malad East area.

Do you take advance table reservations?+

Yes, we strongly recommend reserving in advance, especially for weekends and groups of 4 or more. Use the reservation form on this page or call us at +91 9920599891.

Do you have vegetarian options?+

Absolutely. We have a wide variety of vegetarian dishes including Dal Tadka, Paneer Butter Masala, Shahi Paneer, Veg Biryani, Veg Fried Rice, Veg Noodles, Veg Manchurian, all breads, salads and desserts.

What is the average cost for two people?+

A hearty meal for two typically costs around ₹400, making Cafe Fazal one of Malad's best value-for-money dining experiences without compromising on quality or taste.

Do you cater for private events and parties?+

Yes! We offer private dining arrangements and outside catering for birthdays, anniversaries, corporate events and more. Please call us on +91 9920599891 to discuss your requirements.

Where exactly is Cafe Fazal located?+

We are located near Noorani Masjid on Pathanwadi Road, Kurar Village, Malad East, Mumbai – 400097. We are easily accessible by auto-rickshaw or cab from Malad railway station (Western line).
